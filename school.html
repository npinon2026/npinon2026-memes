It has to be even and exact, that's just an unwritten rule. No one knows what'll happen if you'll get against it and no one dares to try test it. I'd rather just do it at 7:30 to be honest.

How can I be all but number 1? Take my physics exam as an example, 5 minutes in and I just went and calculated what the passing score would be because believe me, I knew I failed.
